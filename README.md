I work with founders and product teams to turn ambiguous AI ideas into usable systems: requirements, workflows, prompts, evaluation loops, launches, and iteration.

This GitHub is where I document how I think about AI products: the problem, the workflow, the model behavior, the evaluation loop, and the product decisions behind the system.

## What I Do

- Translate messy product ideas into clear AI workflows
- Design prompts, rubrics, and evaluation loops
- Build human-in-the-loop systems for quality and trust
- Connect product strategy with launch, measurement, and iteration

## AI Product Portfolio

### [AI Product Portfolio](https://github.com/Mitaliz/ai-product-portfolio)

An anonymized collection of AI product case studies across evaluation, workflow automation, personalization, and growth.

The focus is not production code. The focus is product judgment: how the system should work, where AI belongs, what quality means, and how the product gets better over time.

## How I Think

| Layer | Product question |
|---|---|
| Problem | What painful workflow are we improving? |
| AI role | What should the system classify, generate, recommend, or evaluate? |
| Quality | What does a good output look like? |
| Human loop | Where should people review, override, or improve the system? |
| Growth | What signal tells us this is working? |
